MUN (Model United Nations)

MUN stands for Model United Nations, an educational activity where students simulate the United Nations. It's a popular extracurricular activity for students at all levels, from primary school to university. 

What do students do in MUN? 
        Students simulate UN committees
        They negotiate, debate, and discuss to find solutions to real-world problems
        They research and analyze situations
        They develop public speaking, writing, and presentation skills
        They learn to see matters from other perspectives
Who can participate in MUN? 
        Students from all educational levels, from primary school to university
        Students can participate in conferences in person or online
        
How are MUN conferences organized? 
        Conferences are organized by universities, high schools, non-profit organizations, and other educational groups
        Any school can create a Model UN team and register for a conference
What topics can MUN conferences cover? 
        The Cuban Missile Crisis
        Water wars of 2040
        The fall of the Roman Empire
        Technology giant's data breach
        Ongoing conflict simulation
        First encounter crisis with aliens
